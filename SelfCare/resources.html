Resources

From time to time, they'll be days where we need a little extra help. Help that our loved ones aren't fully able to assist with.

No worries, we've got you covered! Below are a list of great services and organiztions that can help you get a better understanding of yourself, or give you the resources you need.

Check out these great organiztions!

With a Master of Science in health promotion management, Merrit Elizabeth Recovery provides coaching services for women who struggle with eating disorders.
